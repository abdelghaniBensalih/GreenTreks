About US

Why!

GreenTreks exists to transform travel into a meaningful experience that connects people with nature, hidden gems, and communities. We believe in travel that leaves a positive impact on both the traveler and the places visited.

What!

GreenTreks offers unique travel experiences, including adventure tours, cultural immersions, and volunteer projects that focus on environmental conservation and community development.

Mission!

At GreenTreks, our mission is to create unforgettable travel experiences that go beyond sightseeing. We are dedicated to promoting sustainable tourism that connects adventurous travelers with the rich culture and natural beauty of Morocco. Through our eco-friendly travel packages and volunteer opportunities, we aim to make a positive impact on the environment and local communities

How!

We achieve this by curating eco-friendly travel packages, showcasing hidden gems, providing volunteer opportunities, partnering with local communities, and educating travelers on sustainable practices.
Values

  • Community Engagement: We believe in the power of travel to support and uplift local communities. Our partnerships and volunteer projects are designed to provide meaningful contributions.
  • Cultural Respect: We strive to foster genuine connections between travelers and the diverse cultures of Morocco, respecting traditions and customs.
  • Adventure and Exploration: We encourage curiosity and a spirit of adventure, guiding travelers to discover hidden gems and unique experiences off the beaten path.
  • Integrity and Transparency: We operate with honesty and transparency, ensuring that our actions align with our values and that our travelers are well-informed and supported.

Our Story

GreenTreks was born out of a passion for travel and a deep commitment to giving back to our country. Founded by a two founders who are eco-conscious explorers, we saw an opportunity to create travel experiences that not only delight and inspire but also give back to the places we visit.

Our journey began in a quaint village nestled within the High Atlas Mountains, a region known for its breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage.
